FinPulse Market Intelligence

Transforming unstructured social media engagment into decision-grade signals

Core Thesis

FinPulse is built on the thesis that information flow and engagement dynamics increasingly drive price formation across equities, cryptocurrencies, and commodities. In markets defined by real-time news and narrative-driven positioning, price-and-fundamentals-only models are often insufficient for explaining short-horizon dislocations and regime transitions.

How we Convert Attention into Actionable Intelligence

Pillar 01 01 / 04

Model Development Focus

FinPulse structures market attention into measurable components before any predictive interpretation is attempted.

  • Track intensity, velocity, and persistence of market-relevant engagement.
  • Classify engagement by asset class, macro theme, and narrative structure.
  • Evaluate relationships with price, volatility, volume, and liquidity.
Vision

These views represent working hypotheses, not fixed truths. We believe attention shifts often precede price adjustment, and that persistent narratives can outlive the initial news cycle and shape positioning across assets. The first chart tests where engagement leads or diverges from price; the second tracks which themes endure versus decay.
