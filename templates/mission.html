FinPulse Market Intelligence

Transforming unstructured market information into decision-grade signals

Core Thesis

FinPulse is built on the thesis that information flow and engagement dynamics increasingly drive price formation across equities, cryptocurrencies, and commodities. In markets defined by real-time news and narrative-driven positioning, price-and-fundamentals-only models are often insufficient for explaining short-horizon dislocations and regime transitions.

Model Development Focus

  • Track intensity, velocity, and persistence of market-relevant engagement.
  • Classify engagement by asset class, macro theme, and narrative structure.
  • Evaluate relationships with price, volatility, volume, and liquidity.

Why This Signal Matters

Social media increasingly operates as a real-time transmission layer for macro releases, geopolitical developments, corporate events, and trader positioning. Participation from analysts, journalists, policymakers, institutions, and retail cohorts means engagement can both reflect expectations and amplify them.

Structured Indicators

  • Engagement surge flags and sentiment-adjusted momentum.
  • Narrative persistence metrics for medium-term regime tracking.
  • Cross-asset information-sensitivity diagnostics.

Positioning & Roadmap

FinPulse is currently a research-driven, non-commercial initiative prioritising rigorous data handling, transparent assumptions, and analytical discipline. Over time, the framework is intended to extend into advanced alternative data analysis, macro-thematic research, and cross-asset monitoring applications.
